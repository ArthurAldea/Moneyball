## Additional Fix Applied During Verification

**conftest.py autouse Playwright stub** — Merger tests that call `build_dataset →
attach_league_position → scrape_fbref_standings` were hanging because `_playwright_fetch`
launched a real browser on cold cache (no standings CSV). Added `_stub_playwright_default`
autouse fixture in conftest.py to stub `_do_playwright_get` globally. Tests with their
own monkeypatch (`test_backoff_on_429`, `test_column_presence`, `test_standings_scraper_caches`)
override as expected.
